
\subsection{Structural Congruence}
\begin{align*}
P\para 0 \equiv P \equiv 0\para P\\
P\para Q \equiv Q\para P\\
P\para (Q\para R)\equiv (P\para Q)\para R
\end{align*}
The structural congruence for parallel process.
